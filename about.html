Elizabeta Trankulov

I was born on 20th of May, year 1999 and named Elizabeta Trankulov. Bound to live for 19 years in a small town called, in my native language, Stár Bišnov. Though born in the far West of Romania, I am of Bulgarian ethnicity. My town had me educated as a high school graduate at the ”Saints Kiril and Metodii” high school, having a biology & chemistry based curriculum.

I am unable to pinpoint the time I discovered art, it has been pretty early on that I just knew I could draw and paint, I just could. Of course the learning, improving and evolving has no end, but I did not choose art. I can say in a non-poetic note that art chose and claimed me, as I tried to deny it up until I was 16 or 17 years old. After finally accepting the fact that I have no escape, the working phase started, and it shall never end.

Art has you execute things you never thought you would, such as actually pursuing it by choosing an art based college, the subject of graphic design, to be more precise.
